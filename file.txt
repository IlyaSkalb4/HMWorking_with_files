the queen angelfish (Holacanthus ciliaris) is
a species of marine angelfish found 
in the western Atlantic Ocean. 
it is a warm-water species that lives near the 
ocean floor in coral reefs.
it is blue and yellow and has a 
distinctive spot or "crown" on its forehead.
this crown distinguishes it from the similar, 
closely related, Bermuda blue angelfish, 
with which it overlaps in range and can interbreed.
